How to Rock Colors in Life and Design

Check out these simple techniques to use color effectively:

1. Know Your Color Groups

Warm tones (red, orange, yellow):

  • Want a space to feel lively? Use warm tones!
  • Need to stimulate conversation at a party? Decorate with warm tones!
  • Want people to feel hungry? (Restaurant owners, this is for you!)

Cool tones (blue, green, purple):

  • Need focus for work? Surround yourself with cool tones!
  • Want a relaxing bedroom? Choose soft cool tones!
  • Need your website to look trustworthy? Cool tones are your answer!

2. The 60-30-10 Rule: Simple but Effective!

Here's a secret professional designers use:

  • 60% dominant color (most of the space, like walls)
  • 30% secondary color (large furniture pieces)
  • 10% accent color (decorative items, small accessories)

This technique works for home d√©cor, website design, or even outfit planning!
